Systems 4 Life

Our research enables secure and sustainable scaling of computing systems. We tackle the security, sustainability, dependability, and performance challenges of modern and future systems across the system stack with a special focus on computer architecture. We help design fundamentally better systems leveraging security-oriented hardware-software co-design, data-centric security, and testing principles. In addition, our group is interested in building health-oriented systems with a focus on agent-based simulations and genome analysis.
